Benjamin Carter, Ph.D.

Postdoctoral Fellow
Dartmouth College

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at The Nelson A. Rockefeller Center for Public Policy and the Social Sciences where I conduct research and teach courses on American government, public policy, public health, and public opinion. I recieved my PhD in Political Science from Stony Brook University in 2022.

My Research investigates the causes and effects of political disagreement about the proper role of government in promoting a healthy society. My work has been published in scholarly journals such as American Politics Research, JAMA: Oncology, Journal of Urban Health, and Behavior Research Methods.
